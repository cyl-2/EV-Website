Nio, an EV gem

Nio is an amazing Chinese electric vehicle company based in Shanghai, China, founded by William Li in 2014, who started the company to help provide a solution to decrease the pollution caused by gasoline vehicles in the crowded cities of China.
Today, Nio is the world’s seventh-most-valuable EV car maker , with very meaningful goals and ambitions to change how the world transports on a daily basis, leading to a more sustainable, greener future. Nio has plans to debut their cars onto the European market within the next five years which I think is very exciting.

Nio’s name, a special meaning

Nio’s Chinese name, 蔚来 (pronounced as: Weilai) , means Blue Sky Coming. A blue sky indicates a brighter, open minded future. This message is also conveyed through Nio’s logo.

Why choose Nio as the next car to own?

Although Nio cars are not on the European market yet, they should definitely be on your watchlist. Not only does Nio offer these very luxurious, beautiful cars that reduce your carbon footprint, Nio has an amazing plan for customers to ease their worries of maintaining a car - Worry-Free Package. Nio also has a competitive advantage over many other EV cars - BaaS, Battery As A Service.

Nio's Cool Services

Nio, battery as a service (BaaS)

With over 140 battery swap stations in over 60 cities in China, Nio successfully launched BaaS in late August, 2020.

Seeing how BaaS was successfully implemented in many cities throughout China, I am excited to see Nio's development in Europe in the near future.

Main benefits of BaaS:

  • Fully-automatic battery swap in just 3 minutes. Compared to charging a vehicle, which takes more than 6 hours, Nio’s BaaS is clearly a better option.
  • Electric system checks during each swap to ensure the car and battery are at their best performance. This helps the customer to identify any issues with the car (if any) as soon as possible to prevent accidents.
  • BaaS lowers the price of a new vehicle by around 20% if you purchase a car with the service.
  • The customer doesn’t have to worry about the cost of battery replacements or reduced range as the battery ages
  • Customers can use any battery pack based on their needs. All Nio cars are built with the same battery trays so customers can avail of any battery pack with any Nio car. For example, a customer originally has the 70 kWh pack, the customer can also rent the 100 kWh battery if they want to. They just pay the 100 kWh pack lease cost for that month and swap it back when the month is over. Or if they want to continue to use it, they can just continue to pay the higher lease price

Nio, worry-case package

This package is designed to make servicing your Nio vehicle convenient and easy. The initial NIO vehicle owner can enjoy free quality assurance, roadside assistance, and car connectivity services for the duration of the vehicle’s lifetime.

The Worry-Case package includes:

  • Free repairs
  • Free maintenance
  • Free courtesy car provided if your vehicle cannot be repaired in under 24 hours
  • Insurance : Vehicle compulsory insurance, third party liability with zero deductible, passenger insurance, and vehicle damage insurance with zero deductible, are provided.
  • Free car washing service
  • Free valet driver service
  • Free cellular data boost
  • Free airport parking
